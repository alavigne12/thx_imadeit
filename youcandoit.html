You Can Do It!

YOU CAN DO IT! Empowering Archivists to Use Open Tools to Process A/VMaterials was a presentation given at the Northwest Archivists Conference in May 2019. My segment was dedicated to exposing non-experts to the basics of some open source tools for audivosiual digitization as well as for long term digital preservation. I wanted to make the material as friendly and accessible as possible - if people are afraid of the tool, they are much more hesitant to actually use it! My slides, notes, and an expanded definition of an archival information package are included here.
